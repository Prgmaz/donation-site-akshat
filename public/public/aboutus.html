About Us

As we all know right now whole world is going through a pandemic . Due to which lots of people have lost their job especially daily wages workers. This situation is worse for all but it is worst for the workers. Keeping this in mind me and my friends have started an online NGO/donation website named Good Deeds. So basically it is a small initiative taken in order to provide needy people with whatever they need and help them survive this pandemic. So we are going to make it easy for donors to donate the things. So I'll tell you the process quickly how we work. First of all whenever a donor will visit us they will go through our home page which will lead them to donation page where they can select what material they have to fill, accordingly they fill the form also for the donation material we have fixed guidelines, for donation all those should be fulfilled. After it is donor will receive a pop up of thanks for donating and keep doing great work.Also in case donor isn't able to reach us for donations we will make sure that our volunteers go and pick up the material with all safety measures taken. In the end when the donation is done i.e things are given to needy our donators will receive a confirmation via email. That's a small step taken by us for the development and yeah ofcourse for needy people.
